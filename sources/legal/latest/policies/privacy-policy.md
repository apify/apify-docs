PLEASE READ THIS PRIVACY POLICY CAREFULLY TO UNDERSTAND HOW WE HANDLE YOUR PERSONAL DATA. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY, PLEASE DO NOT USE OUR WEBSITE OR SERVICES.

## When the Privacy Policy applies

This Privacy Policy applies where Apify acts as a data controller of your Personal Data. This includes when you:
- Visit or interact with our Website or branded social media pages;
- Create or manage an Account on the Platform;
- Use our Services where we act as a controller of your Personal Data;
- Register for or attend our events, webinars, conferences, or training sessions;
- Communicate with us, including through support, sales, or other inquiry channels;
- Receive communications from us;
- Apply to a job offer at Apify;
- Are employed by or work for a customer or partner who interacts with us in a context where Apify acts as a controller (for example, during the sales or contracting process);
- Participate in surveys, research, or contests facilitated by us; or
- Interact with our community, including the Apify Store, public forums, or developer resources.

## When the Privacy Policy does not apply

- **Apify as a data processor**. In case you decide to collect and manage Personal Data when using the Platform or other Services, Apify is a "**data processor**," not a "**data controller**" (as defined by applicable privacy laws), of the Personal Data we process under your instructions and on your behalf. This Privacy Policy does not apply to such processing activities. They are governed by [the Apify Data Processing Addendum](../terms/data-processing-addendum.md) (or a separately executed data processing agreement) between Apify and you. For detailed privacy information about how a specific Apify customer handles Personal Data processed through our Services, please contact that customer directly. We are not responsible for our customers' privacy or data security practices, which may differ from those described here.
- **Employees and contractors**. This Privacy Policy does not apply to Personal Data about current and former Apify employees, or contractors and agents acting in similar roles, which is governed by separate internal notices.
- **Third-party services**. Our Website and Platform may contain links to third-party websites, applications, and services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to the information practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before interacting with them.

## Personal Data we collect

### Personal Data you provide to us

We collect Personal Data that you provide directly to us. The types of data we collect depend on how you interact with us and may include:
- **Account and profile information**. When you create a user Account on the Platform, we collect your name, email address, username, password, and other information you provide us with. You may also voluntarily provide a short bio, homepage URL, GitHub username, social media usernames, and a profile picture, which may be displayed on your public profile on the Platform.
- **Billing and payment information**. When you purchase paid Services, we collect billing information such as your name, billing address, and payment details. We use secure third-party payment service providers to process payments.
- **Communications and support information**. When you contact us (including through email, chat, support tickets, or social media channels), we collect the content of your communications, your contact details, and any other information you choose to provide.
- **Event and registration information**. When you register for or attend our events, webinars, conferences, or training sessions, we collect registration information such as your name, email address, company name, job title, and any other information provided during registration.
- **Job application information**. When you apply for a position at Apify, we collect information about your professional career and educational background, including your name, contact details, current and previous job positions, degrees, qualifications, resume or CV, and cover letter.
- **Survey and feedback information**. When you participate in surveys, provide feedback, or submit testimonials, we collect the information you provide as part of that activity.
- **Public forum and community contributions**. When you post reviews, issues, comments, or other content on Apify Store, community forums, blogs, or other publicly accessible areas of our Services, we collect the information you submit. See the "Public forums and Actor Reviews" section for more details.
- **Verification data**. In certain circumstances, we may ask you to verify your identity. If you choose to do so, data we will collect includes, depending on the method: an image of your government-issued identity document and the information appearing on it (such as your ID number and date of birth); your image in photo or video form, facial geometry templates; and the result of the verification.


### Personal Data we collect through automated means

Like most websites and technology services, we automatically collect certain information when you visit our Website and use our Services. This information may include:
- **Device and connection information**. Device name, operating system type and version, browser type and version, language settings, unique device identifiers, and screen resolution.
- **Network and access information**. IP address, Internet service provider, hostname, and approximate geographic location inferred from your IP address.
- **Usage information**. Date and time of access, pages and features viewed, actions taken within the Platform, search queries, referring and exit URLs, session duration, clicks, and interactions with content and features.
- **Logs**. Connection logs and statistics about data sent to and from other devices in connection with your use of the Services.

### Cookies

When you visit our Website or use our Services, we may collect Personal Data through cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar technologies. These technologies help us provide and improve our Services, analyze usage, personalize content, and deliver relevant advertising. If you provide us with Personal Data, cookies may act as an identifier that ties your information to your device or IP address.

Other than cookies necessary for the Website to function, you can choose not to allow cookies. For detailed information about the cookies we use, their purposes, and how to manage your preferences, please see our [Cookie Policy](cookie-policy.md).

### Personal Data we collect from other sources

We may also obtain Personal Data about you from other sources, including:
- **Third-party data providers**. We may receive business contact information (such as name, email address, job title, company name, and professional profile information) from third-party data providers and publicly available sources to support our sales, marketing, and business development activities.
- **Advertising and analytics partners**. We may receive information about your interactions with our advertisements on third-party platforms, including conversion data and audience insights.
- **Third-party integrations and sign-on services**. If you create or log into your Account using a third-party service (such as Google or GitHub), we receive certain information from that service, such as your name and email address, as permitted by your settings with that third-party service. The information we receive depends on the settings, permissions, and privacy policy of the third-party service. You should review the privacy settings and notices of those services to understand what data may be disclosed to us.
- **Event partners and co-marketing partners**. If you interact with us at events hosted or co-sponsored by our partners, we may receive registration or contact information from those partners.

We may combine Personal Data collected from these sources with information we collect through other means described in this Privacy Policy.

## How we use your Personal Data

We process your Personal Data for the following purposes:

- **Providing and administering the Website and Services**
    - Authenticating you and providing access to the Platform and Services;
    - Processing payments and managing your subscription;
    - Creating and maintaining your Account and publicly visible Apify profile (populated only with information you choose to provide);
    - Providing customer support and responding to your requests, questions, and feedback.

- **Improving and developing our Services**
    - Assessing trends, usage patterns, and user behavior across the Website and Platform to determine what new features, integrations, or improvements may be of interest to users;
    - Conducting research and analysis regarding the use of the Services;
    - Testing new features and functionality;
    - Diagnosing and resolving technical issues.

- **Marketing and promotional communications**
    - Contacting you by email, postal mail, or phone with news, updates, promotions, surveys, or contests relating to the Services or other offerings that may be of interest to you, in accordance with applicable legal requirements;
    - Customizing and optimizing the content and advertising you see on our Website and on third-party platforms;
    - Uploading hashed customer identifiers (such as email addresses) to advertising platforms (including Google, LinkedIn, X, and TikTok) for the purpose of creating custom audiences, delivering conversion signals, excluding existing customers from acquisition campaigns, and measuring advertising effectiveness;
    - Using cookie-based and pixel-based retargeting technologies on our Website and third-party platforms to deliver relevant advertising to visitors and users.

- **Security, fraud prevention, and abuse detection**
    - Monitoring and protecting against unauthorized access, security incidents, fraud, and other malicious, deceptive, or illegal activity;
    - Detecting and preventing violations of our legal terms and policies;
    - Protecting the rights, property, or safety of Apify, its users, and the public.

- **Recruiting**
    - Evaluating and selecting job applicants, including setting up and conducting interviews and assessments;
    - Including you in our talent pool and contacting you should a suitable position become available, if you have consented to this.

- **Legal compliance and enforcement**
    - Complying with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, and enforceable governmental requests;
    - Establishing, exercising, or defending our legal rights;
    - Enforcing our agreements.

## How we disclose your Personal Data

- **Service providers (our data processors)**. We provide access to or disclose your Personal Data to selected third-party service providers who help us operate our Website, provide the Platform, and deliver our Services. These include providers of billing and payment processing, advertising and marketing, analytics and research, customer support, data storage and hosting, security and fraud prevention, web hosting, AI tools, applicant tracking, and legal services. These service providers process your Personal Data on our behalf and under our instructions, and are contractually required to protect it.
- **Advertising and analytics partners**. We disclose Personal Data to advertising networks and analytics providers that assist us in measuring, improving, and optimizing our marketing campaigns and website performance. This includes:
    - Uploading hashed customer identifiers (such as email addresses) to advertising platforms including Google, LinkedIn, X (formerly Twitter), and TikTok for custom audience matching, conversion tracking, audience exclusions, and campaign optimization;
    - Allowing third-party cookies, pixels, and similar technologies on our Website to collect information about your browsing behavior for interest-based advertising and analytics purposes.
    
    This disclosure may constitute "sharing" of Personal Data for purposes of cross-context behavioral advertising under the California Consumer Privacy Act ("**CCPA**") and similar US state laws.

- **Actor Creators**. When you run a third-party Actor (a program developed by a third-party developer, referred to as a "Creator") through the Platform, the Actor operates under your Account and may access certain data within your Account, depending on the permission level it requires:
    - **Limited permissions** (default). The Actor has restricted access, primarily to its own storages, the data it generates, and resources you explicitly provide to it. It cannot access other data in your Account.
    - **Full permissions**. The Actor has access to all data in your Account.
    
    Each Actor's required permission level is displayed in its detail page in Apify Store. When you run a third-party Actor, the Actor Creator may receive or have access to Personal Data in connection with providing the Actor's functionality. You control which Actors you run and what permission level they receive. Wherever possible, we recommend choosing Actors that use limited permissions. We strongly encourage you to review the Actor Creator's privacy practices before running their Actors, particularly those requesting full permissions.
- **Protection of Apify and others**. We reserve the right to use or disclose your Personal Data if required by law or if we reasonably believe that use or disclosure is necessary to: (a) comply with applicable law, regulation, court order, or other legal process; (b) enforce our agreements; (c) protect the rights, property, or personal safety of Apify, its employees, its users, or the public; (d) detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues; or (e) respond to a valid legal request from a public authority.
- **Joint offerings and partners**. From time to time, Apify may partner with other companies to offer products or services jointly. If you purchase or specifically express interest in a jointly offered product or service, we may share certain Personal Data with our joint promotion partner(s). Apify does not control its business partners' use of the Personal Data we share with them, and their use is governed by their own privacy policies. If you do not wish for your Personal Data to be shared in connection with joint offerings, you may opt not to purchase or express interest in such offerings.
- **Business transfers**. ## How we retain and dispose your Personal Data

We retain your Personal Data for no longer than necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When we no longer have a legitimate business need or legal basis to process your Personal Data, we will either delete it or anonymize it. If deletion is not immediately possible (for example, because Personal Data has been stored in backup archives), we will securely isolate it from further processing until deletion is possible.

## Territory-specific terms

### EEA and the UK

**Your data protection rights**. Under applicable data protection laws in the EEA and the UK, you may exercise the following rights:
- Right to access. You have the right to obtain confirmation of whether we process your Personal Data, and to obtain a copy of it.
- Right to data portability. You may receive Personal Data that you have provided to us in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and transmit it to another data controller without hindrance, where the processing is based on your consent or a contract and is carried out by automated means.
- Right to rectification. You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate Personal Data and the completion of incomplete data.
- Right to object. You have the right to object to the processing of your Personal Data where we rely on legitimate interest as a legal basis. Where you object to processing for direct marketing purposes, we will cease such processing without requiring you to provide a specific reason.
- Right to restrict processing. In certain cases, you may request that we restrict the processing of your Personal Data (for example, where you contest its accuracy or where processing is unlawful but you oppose erasure).
- Right to erasure. You may request that we erase your Personal Data in certain circumstances (for example, where it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected).
- Right to lodge a complaint. You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. For users located in the Czech Republic, the competent supervisory authority is the Office for Personal Data Protection (Úřad pro ochranu osobních údajů, ÚOOÚ), Pplk. Sochora 27, 170 00 Prague 7, Czech Republic, www.uoou.cz. If you are located in another EEA member state or the UK, you may also lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority in your country of habitual residence or place of work.
- Right to refuse or withdraw consent. If we ask for your consent to process your Personal Data, you are free to refuse. If you have given your consent, you may withdraw it at any time without adverse consequences. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out prior to the withdrawal.
- Right not to be subject to automated decision-making. The types of solely automated decision-making referred to in Article 22(1) and (4) of the EU/UK GDPR do not currently take place in connection with your Personal Data. Should this change, we will inform you about the logic involved, the significance, and the possible consequences. You will also have the right to human intervention, to express your point of view, and to contest the decision.

**Profiling**. Where we use your Personal Data for audience matching, personalization of advertising content, or behavioral segmentation (as described above), this may constitute profiling within the meaning of Article 4(4) of the GDPR. This profiling does not produce legal effects or similarly significantly affect you. You have the right to object to such profiling at any time by contacting us at privacy@apify.com or by using the cookie preference settings on our Website.

Certain Personal Data is necessary to enter into and perform our contract with you (for example, your name, email address, and payment information are needed to create your Account and provide you with the Services). - Geolocation data (approximate location inferred from IP address);
- Professional or employment-related information (such as job title, company name);
- Education information (where provided in connection with a job application or eligibility verification);
- Inferences drawn from the above categories.

**No "sale" of Personal Data**. We do not "sell" Personal Data as that term is conventionally understood (i.e., we do not disclose Personal Data in exchange for monetary consideration).
**Sensitive Personal Information**. We do not collect or use sensitive personal information (as defined under the CCPA) for purposes of inferring characteristics about you.

**How to exercise your rights**. To exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@apify.com. We will verify your identity using the information associated with your Account. You may also designate an authorized agent to exercise these rights on your behalf; we may require the agent to provide proof of authorization and may need to verify your identity directly.
