For when you want data or automation from an existing tool in [Apify Store](https://apify.com/store), without writing any code. Follow the tutorials to get your first result, then use the concept pages to understand what you ran.

## Follow the tutorials

- [Run your first Actor](/actors/running) — choose an Actor, provide input, run it, and export the results.
- [Getting started course](/academy/getting-started) — a guided walkthrough in Apify Academy, from creating an account to your first run.
- [Try an Actor hands-on](/academy/getting-started/actors) — run an Actor step by step and explore its output in Apify Console.

## Learn the concepts

- [What's an Actor?](/actors) — the input → task → output model.
- [Actors in Store](/actors/running/actors-in-store) — how to choose an Actor and the pricing models you'll see (pay per event, pay per usage, rental).
- [Input and output](/actors/running/input-and-output) — how to configure a run and where its results go.
- [Runs and builds](/actors/running/runs-and-builds) — run states and how to resurrect a finished run.
- [Storage](/storage) — where your results live: [datasets](/storage/dataset) for structured results and the [key-value store](/storage/key-value-store) for files and records.
- [Usage and resources](/actors/running/usage-and-resources) — what a run consumes and how you're billed.

## Where to next

- [Save your input as a task](/actors/running/tasks) so you can re-run without re-typing it.
- [Schedule an Actor](/actors/running/schedules) to run automatically.
- [Use an Actor as a live API](/actors/running/standby) with Actor Standby.
- [Send results to your tools](/integrations) — Make, Zapier, n8n, Slack, webhooks, and more.
- [Monitor your runs](/actors/running/monitoring).
- Run programmatically via the [API](/api/v2) or the [CLI](/cli/docs).
- Ready to build your own? Switch to the [Build Actors](/get-started/learning-paths/build-actors) path.
